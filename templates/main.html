Welcome! سلام!


This website contains a series of lessons on the Arabic alphabet, originally developed by Ahmad. Navid edited, arranged, and compiled the lessons into the format of a website. There are a total of 23 lessons, which are then grouped into four chapters. You may do one chapter per day, or less or more as you wish.

There are a set of PDF slides which go along with the lessons. Please click on the "Slides" link at left to access them. Additionally, there is a chart which shows how the shape of the letters changes depending on their position within a word. You may access this via the "Shape Chart" link. Finally, if you wish to hear the pronunciation of the different letters, there are many videos on YouTube. Best of luck in your learning.
