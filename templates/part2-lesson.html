Lesson 11

Lesson 11, page 15. The next two letters should be studied together. They are like two opposite twin brothers. The one in page 15 is HA. it's pronounced like a heavy h. Or to be more accurate like the sound you make when you blow air from your throat into your hands during winter when it's cold, to make it warm. That's why this letter is related to warmth, heat and feelings as we shall see. Arabic uses the whole range of sounds we can produce, from the top of the tongue back till the middle of the throat. This letter comes from the deepest place! I guess that's why it's related to emotions. There are many words that start with this letter which convey the meanings of heat and feelings. حب love. حرب war. (heat of the battle!). حفلة party. (hot party!). Hhobb=love, Hharb=war. Hhafla=party. Also Hharara=heat. Hhatab=firewood. Hhayy=Alive. Hhorreyya= freedom. Hhess=emotion. Hhanaan=kindness. FaraHh=wedding. Or=happiness. JarHh=wound (can be emotional wound).

So as you can see that letter is unique Subhanallah. If you pronounce it right you will feel that it's coming from deep inside your throat, actually from a deeper place in your lungs near your heart!. So it is a very expressive letter. In the other hand his twin brother is a loser, that's why we differentiate him by an added dot. The sound here is more superficial. Coming from the top of the throat. Like the hocking sound when people spit! And look the ugly insensitive words related to this letter Kha in contrast with its twin Hha. Kha'ef= afraid. Kha'en=traitor. Kharaab=destruction. Khatima=ending. Khaser=loser. Khabeeth=cunning. Khadsh=scratch (superficial!). Khed'a=trick. Khorda=scrap. Kharif=autumn. Khodoo'a=yielding. Khateer=dangerous.

Of course not all the words that have that letter are negative, but this is the general feel of the letter even the sound is a bit ugly! Study these two contrasting twin letters together and you will never forget them.
